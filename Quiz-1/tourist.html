Tourist Spots

Senggigi Beach

Senggigi Beach is one of the beach tourist attractions in the west of Lombok Island. This beach is located in Senggigi Village, Batu Layar District, West Lombok Regency.

Mount Rinjani

Mount Rinjani is a mountain located on the island of Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara. The mountain is the second highest volcano in Indonesia and is a favorite mountain for Indonesian climbers because of its scenic beauty.
